Get Individual ETF/Fund

Get Product by ISIN

This endpoint searches for an ETF/Fund by its ISIN

Retrieves all of the information known for a specific product in a JSON format.

Parameters

  • isin String Mandatory

    The ISIN that distinctly identifies the product.

Examples

  • GET https://test-api.zurianalytics.com/fund/DE000ETFL326

    Retrieves information about Deka MSCI China. Not authenticated. There is a daily maximum.

  • GET https://test-api.zurianalytics.com/fund/LU0392496690&token=eyJ0..

    Retrieves information about ComStage ATX UCITS ETF
